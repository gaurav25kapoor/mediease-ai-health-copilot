# MediEase – AI Health Copilot & Smart Triage Platform

MediEase is an AI-powered healthcare assistant that analyzes medical reports, predicts disease risks, performs intelligent symptom triage, and provides a context-aware AI health copilot through a modern analytics dashboard.

The platform combines machine learning models, LLM reasoning, and medical data analysis to help users understand their health risks and medical reports.

---

## Features

### Medical Report Analyzer
- Upload lab reports
- OCR extracts medical values
- AI interprets lab results
- Risk score generation
- Reports stored in database

### Disease Risk Prediction
Machine learning models predict risks for:
- Anemia
- Diabetes
- Kidney Disease
- Heart Disease

Predictions are stored and visualized in the dashboard.

### AI Symptom Triage
Users describe symptoms in natural language.
